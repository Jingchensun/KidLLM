You are asked to come up with a set of 20 diverse task instructions about
identifying whether the speaker is native to UK or a non-native of the UK from
the audio.
Here are the requirements:
1. These instructions should be to instruct someone to simply ask if the
speaker is a native to UK or a non-native of the UK. The answer is that the
speaker is either a native to UK or a non-native of UK. Avoid mentioning the
gender or the words meaning 'gender' in the questions.
Don't make the questions about anything else.
2. Try not to repeat the verb for each instruction to maximize diversity.
3. The language used for instruction also should be diverse. For example, you
should combine questions with imperative instructions.
4. The type of instructions should be diverse.
5. The instructions should be in English.
6. The instructions should be 1 sentence long. Either an imperative sentence
or a question is permitted.
7. The tasks should be synonymous to "Identify from the audio whether the
speaker is a native or a non-native of the UK "
